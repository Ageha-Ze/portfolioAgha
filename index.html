// QA & Testing

Manual Test Discipline

Spent years testing mobile games at Gameloft. Titles like Asphalt 9, Disney Magic Kingdom, Pastry, Ice Age Village, and The Blacklist passed through my hands before they shipped. Got trusted with Ads Testing along the way and moved up to Junior 2.

Since 2023, freelancing as a Software QA Tester across Testbirds, Test IO, Tester Work, and uTest. Picked up a few "Tester of the Week" wins and rated-tester badges here and there.

✦ Building & Business

From Honey to Code

Running Sabrina Honey since 2019, sourcing raw honey straight from local beekeepers and packing it for resellers and retail shelves. Before that, tried my hand at herbal retail, bookstore marketing, and even ran a small restaurant for a while.

In 2025 started building my own products using AI-assisted development ("vibe coding"): a POS system for UMKM, a Flutter Qur'an app, an Umrah companion app, and a few client websites. Learned architecture, deployment, and Supabase mostly by just doing it.

“ "Looking for the next opportunity to grow further in software testing and AI-assisted product development, happy to jump into projects that actually matter to the people using them. Open to any kind of work, really, as long as it helps the company, stays within what's agreed with no exploitation, and lets me pick up new skills along the way."
